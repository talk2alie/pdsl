The Petroleum (Exploration & Production) Act, 2011

Being an act to provide for the management of petroleum operations, to regulate and promote petroleum exploration, development and production; to regulate the licensing and participation of commercial entities in petroleum operations; to provide for proper supervision of petroleum operations, to promote the participation of Sierra Leone in the petroleum industry.

The Sierra Leone Data Room Rules

The Petroleum Directorate Sierra Leone (PDSL) will make available via a Data Room technical data packages including well data, seismic data and technical reports relating to, and in preparation for, future investment opportunities in Sierra Leone.

The London PDSL Data Room Rules

Getech Group, on behalf of the Petroleum Directorate Sierra Leone (PDSL), will make available via a London Data Room technical data packages including well data, seismic data, and technical reports relating to, and in preparation for, future investment opportunities in Sierra Leone.
